CAS 330

The Auditor Responses to Assessed Risks

Audit Engagement Reference Document

Objective

  • Obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence through designing and implementing responses to RMM

Key Requirements

  • Design overall responses to address assessed RMM
  • Perform tests of controls when relying on those controls
  • Perform substantive procedures for each material class of transactions
  • For significant risks: perform substantive procedures responsive to the risk
