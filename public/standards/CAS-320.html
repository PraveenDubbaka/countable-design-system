CAS 320

Materiality in Planning and Performing an Audit

Audit Engagement Reference Document

Objective

  • Apply the concept of materiality appropriately in planning and performing the audit

Key Requirements

  • Determine overall materiality for the financial statements as a whole
  • Determine performance materiality
  • Determine specific materiality for particular classes of transactions
  • Revise materiality as the audit progresses if circumstances change
