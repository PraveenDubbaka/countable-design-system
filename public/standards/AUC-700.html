AU-C 700

Forming an Opinion and Reporting on Financial Statements (AICPA)

Audit Engagement Reference Document

Objective

  • Form an opinion on the financial statements and express it in a written report

Key Requirements

  • Evaluate whether FS are presented fairly in accordance with the framework
  • Form unmodified or modified opinion
  • PCAOB requirements apply for public company audits
