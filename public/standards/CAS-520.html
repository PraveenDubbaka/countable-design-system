CAS 520

Analytical Procedures

Audit Engagement Reference Document

Objective

  • Obtain relevant and reliable audit evidence using substantive analytical procedures
  • Design and perform final analytical procedures near end of audit

Key Requirements

  • Develop an expectation of recorded amounts or ratios
  • Determine an acceptable difference without further investigation
  • Compare recorded amounts with the expectation
  • Investigate significant differences and corroborate with other evidence
