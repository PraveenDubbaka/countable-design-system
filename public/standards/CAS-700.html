CAS 700

Forming an Opinion and Reporting on Financial Statements

Audit Engagement Reference Document

Objective

  • Form an opinion on whether the financial statements are presented fairly
  • Express the opinion clearly through a written report

Key Requirements

  • Conclude on whether sufficient appropriate audit evidence has been obtained
  • Evaluate whether financial statements are free from material misstatement
  • Report includes: opinion, basis, key audit matters, other responsibilities

Key Terms

Unmodified OpinionModified OpinionKey Audit MattersEmphasis of Matter
